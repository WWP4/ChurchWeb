Vision

A Church that Heals People and Heals Places

We designed Kingdom Builders to turn compassion into capacity. Your teams shepherd people through spiritual healing, then train them in work, leadership, and service—so families stabilize, small businesses launch, and neighborhoods feel the difference.

Framework

Restore • Equip • Release

Three movements that repeat each quarter—lightweight to run, deep enough to transform.

1 • Restore

Healing & Foundations

Trauma‑informed small groups, prayer and counsel, and a simple rhythm of Scripture, reflection, and community care. Build safety and belonging first.

2 • Equip

Skills & Apprenticeship

Workshops on money, communication, and entrepreneurship. Practical labs and mentor matching. Participants practice, ship, and get feedback.

3 • Release

Service & Venture

Graduates serve on teams, launch micro‑ventures, or join community projects. We measure impact—jobs, hours served, families helped.

How it Runs

90‑Day Cycle, Repeatable All Year

Weeks 1–3 • Welcome & Restore

Story, safety, and spiritual foundations. Baseline care plan for each participant.

Weeks 4–8 • Equip & Practice

Weekly workshops and labs. Mentor check‑ins. Real deliverables each week.

Weeks 9–12 • Release & Celebrate

Service placements, micro‑venture launches, testimony Sunday. New leaders identified for the next cycle.
